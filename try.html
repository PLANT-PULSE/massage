Our Services

Experience a variety of massage therapies designed to meet your wellness needs

Swedish Massage

A gentle form of massage that uses long strokes, kneading, and circular movements to help you relax and energize.

  • Duration: 45/60 minutes
  • Benefits: Relaxation, improved circulation
  • Price: ₵450 (45min) / ₵500 (60min)
Book Now

Deep Tissue Massage

Targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue to relieve chronic patterns of tension.

  • Duration: 45/60 minutes
  • Benefits: Pain relief, muscle tension release
  • Price: ₵500 (45min) / ₵600 (60min)
Book Now

Holistic Massage

Uses heated stones to enhance relaxation and loosen tight muscles for a deeply soothing experience.

  • Duration: 45/60 minutes
  • Benefits: Deep relaxation, muscle tension relief
  • Price: ₵450 (45min) / ₵550 (60min)
Book Now

Couples Massage

Enjoy side-by-side massages with your partner in a private room for a shared relaxing experience.

  • Duration: 90 minutes
  • Benefits: Shared experience, relaxation
  • Price: ₵1100
Book Now

Reflexology

Experience the healing power of reflexology. This ancient therapy applies pressure to specific points on your feet.

  • Duration: 30 minutes
  • Benefits: Stress relief, improved circulation
  • Price: Contact for pricing
Book Now

Pedicure & Manicure

Pamper your hands and feet with our professional nail care services including exfoliation and moisturizing.

  • Duration: 60 minutes each
  • Benefits: Nail care, hand & foot treatment
  • Price: ₵270 each
Book Now
